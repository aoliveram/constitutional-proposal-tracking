## 3. Notable Patterns
- **High Activity Reports**: Reports 1, 6, and 7 represent the peaks of modification activity for this commission.
- **Structural Changes**: A significant number of indications (~50) target non-article elements such as "Capítulos" or "Títulos".
- **Renumbering**: Several indications explicitly mention renumbering (e.g., "Art 4 passes to be Art 3"), which is recorded in the `target_article` field.
